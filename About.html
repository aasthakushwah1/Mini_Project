About Safety+

Safety brings first aid to the uninjured

Safety + is a Safety and Security-based Website for Women. One of application is when a Women would be travelling from one place to another, this website will ensure their safety by using real-time location.This website will also contain self-defense tutorials for every age group and will also make sure to reach out to those Women who are suffering from domestic voilence and help them through various means.

Safety+ is expressly focused on the safety of the all womens around by using the technologies .Women's safety is one of the biggest concerns which has taken over the world for a few generations. But, as time is moving, and technology is evolving, you can see many new contributions with the same goal.

Safety+ aims is to acquaint girls with basic self-defence moves that will help them when in trouble. The goal is to help them develop a successful plan before something happens. Basic self defence moves given on the website have been designed by professionals who have years of experience in creating self-defence programs.

This women's safety website can be a perfect companion to keep you safe. The website lets you add up to 5 contacts for emergency alerts. You can send them text messages along with the Google Map location.
